How to Play

/ Take down other players to reach the #1 spot, win stairs and dynamite. / Build stairs to reach high ground. / Throw dynamite to destroy everything around! / Farm trees to loot stairs. / Headshots will give you more loot.

Controls

  • WASD: move
  • SPACE: jump
  • LEFT-CLICK: use item
  • 1,2,3,4,5: switch item

(You can edit keybinds in options)
